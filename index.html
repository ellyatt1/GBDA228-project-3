GBDA 101: Logo Design

Details of The Project:

The Task was to design a logo following a client brief from a classmate. The logo would need to follow and somehow convey what the clients interests and hobbies which were outlined in the client brief

The client outlined in the brief that they love to read and really like fantasy world building. My goal with this logo was to combine both of these together into a seamless logo that conveys those two interests the client specifically pointed out.

Requirements

  • 3 sketches of logo ideas
  • One logo design presented in colour aswell as Black and White
  • Must communicate the clients interest and hobbies

Skills Acquired:

  • Beginner experience with Adobe Illustrator
  • Beginner experience following a client brief
  • Beginner experience in graphic design and branding

Software Used

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
